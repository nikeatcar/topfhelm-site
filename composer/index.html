How Commissions Work

01

Send Lore & References

Describe your project, character, world, scene, mood and musical references.

02

Discuss Atmosphere

We define the tone, structure, instruments and emotional direction.

03

Sketch Phase

A first concept or musical direction is created before full production.

04

Composition

The track is arranged, orchestrated and shaped into a full soundtrack piece.

05

Revisions

Adjustments are made according to the selected commission tier.

06

Delivery

You receive final WAV and MP3 files ready for use in your project.
